About Essendon Accounting

Founded in 2010, Essendon Accounting emerged from a vision to provide personalized, innovative financial solutions to local businesses and individuals. Our journey began with a small team of dedicated professionals who believed in combining traditional accounting expertise with modern technology to deliver exceptional service.

Our mission is to empower our clients with financial clarity and confidence. We're driven by core values of integrity, innovation, and client success, ensuring every interaction adds value to your financial journey. Through continuous learning and adaptation to changing market conditions, we remain at the forefront of accounting excellence.

Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer for small businesses?

We provide comprehensive services including bookkeeping, tax planning, BAS lodgment, cloud accounting setup, financial reporting, and business advisory services tailored to small business needs.

How can I switch to your cloud accounting platform?

Our team handles the entire transition process, including data migration, staff training, and ongoing support. The switch typically takes 2-3 business days with minimal disruption to your operations.

What are your fees and payment terms?

We offer flexible payment plans and transparent pricing based on your specific needs. Most services can be bundled into monthly packages, and we provide detailed quotes before commencing any work.

How often should we meet to review my finances?

We recommend quarterly reviews for most clients, but we can adjust the frequency based on your business size and complexity. All clients receive monthly reports and unlimited email support.

What tax planning strategies do you recommend?

We develop customized tax strategies based on your specific situation, focusing on legal deductions, timing of income and expenses, structure optimization, and long-term tax efficiency.
